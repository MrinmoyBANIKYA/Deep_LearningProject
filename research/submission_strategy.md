# IEEE Conference Submission Strategy

## 🏆 Recommended Conferences (Free/Low-Fee)
1.  **IEEE ICMLA** (International Conference on Machine Learning and Applications)
2.  **IEEE ICCMC** (International Conference on Computing Methodologies and Communication)
3.  **IEEE TENCON** (Premier IEEE Region 10 Conference - very prestigious for Asia-Pacific)

## 📅 Timeline & Deadlines
- Most international conferences have deadlines **4–6 months** before the event.
- **Submission window**: Usually Open from Jan–March for Year-end conferences.
- **Check**: Use [EasyChair](https://easychair.org/) or [EDAS](https://edas.info/) to find upcoming IEEE conferences in India.

## 📝 How to Apply
1.  **Latex/Word Template**: Download the official [IEEE Conference Template](https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html).
2.  **Overleaf**: Upload your `manuscript.md` content into an Overleaf IEEE project.
3.  **Anonymization**: Most conferences are "Double-Blind." Remove your name and institution from the PDF before submitting.
4.  **Submission Portal**: Create an account on the conference's submission site (EDAS/EasyChair) and upload your PDF.

## 💡 Pro-Tips for 2nd Year UGs
- **Get a Faculty Mentor**: Having a professor's name as the last author significantly increases acceptance chances.
- **Plagiarism Check**: Use Turnitin (usually available via your college library). Aim for **<15% similarity**.
- **IEEE Student Membership**: Join IEEE (costs ~$27/year for students). It gives you massive discounts on conference registrations.
